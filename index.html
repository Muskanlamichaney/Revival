About Us

Revival Enterprises is an independent Entity which functions to Support and Invest in the Kingdom of God. Unlike other businesses focused solely on profit, Revival Enterprises aims to provide services and employment, uplifting those losing hope and vision. The Team Revival, led by Mr. Sornim Tamang, manages all aspects of the business, including establishment works, branch management, and financial management.

Revival has various concepts and sectors in business to adapt to the changing business environment.
